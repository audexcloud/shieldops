// FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before we get started.

Do I need to already have Wiz or other security tools? +
No. We're tool-agnostic. If you already have Wiz, Prisma Cloud, or AWS-native security services, we'll work within your existing tooling. If you don't have a CSPM yet, we'll recommend the right fit for your size and budget — and handle the full deployment.
How long does it take to become audit-ready? +
Most clients are audit-ready in 6-10 weeks from kickoff, depending on the size of their environment and the target framework. Phase 1 (audit) takes 1-2 weeks, and Phase 2 (build) takes 4-8 weeks. We've had clients pass their first SOC 2 Type II audit within 3 months of engagement start.
What does "AI-driven remediation" actually mean? +
We build automated workflows that use AI to analyze security findings, determine the correct remediation action, and execute it — without human intervention. For example, if a new S3 bucket is created without encryption, the system detects it, classifies the risk, applies the correct Terraform remediation, and closes the ticket in ServiceNow. Your team only gets involved for edge cases that require judgment.
Do you work with environments outside AWS? +
Our core expertise is AWS, which is where we deliver the most value. That said, the compliance automation patterns we build are cloud-agnostic at the methodology level. If you have a multi-cloud environment, we can discuss how to extend the approach.
What happens after the engagement ends? +
Everything we build is yours — Terraform modules, CI/CD configurations, automation workflows, and documentation. If you choose not to continue with our managed retainer, your team can operate the system independently. We design for handoff from day one so you're never locked in.
How is ShieldOps different from a traditional security consultancy? +
Traditional firms sell you auditors who produce reports. We're engineers who build systems. The output isn't a PDF of findings — it's working infrastructure that continuously enforces your security and compliance posture. Our automation-first approach means you get better coverage at a fraction of the ongoing cost.
